Sprunki: The Classic Arcade Challenge

Experience the thrill of this fast-paced, two-player retro game from 1996.

How to Play Sprunki

Sprunki is an exciting two-player arcade game that combines quick reflexes with strategic thinking. Players control characters that must navigate through platforms, collect power-ups, and outsmart their opponent to win. The simple controls make it easy to pick up, but mastering the game takes practice and skill.

Game Controls

Player 1: WASD keys for movement

Player 2: Arrow keys for movement

Special Moves: Space bar (P1) and Enter (P2)

Game Features

  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Power-ups and special abilities
  • Retro pixel art graphics
  • Original arcade soundtrack

The History of Sprunki

Released in 1996, Sprunki quickly became a favorite in arcades around the world. Its unique combination of platforming and competitive gameplay made it stand out among other arcade titles of its time. The game was created by a small indie development team that wanted to bring the joy of couch co-op gaming to arcade cabinets.

Over the years, Sprunki has maintained a dedicated fanbase, with tournaments still being held to this day. This online version brings the classic arcade experience to your browser, allowing you to enjoy this retro gem without needing the original hardware.
